## Compound datatypes in Rust 

 ## There are majorly 5 Compound datatypes in Rust 

  - Arrays
  - Tuples
  - Slices 
  - Strings
  - Slice String


## Important Note 

  # There are two display formats in Rust
   - Debuggable Format  (:?)
   - Display Format (Default)


## Arrays

 - Fixed sized collection of elements of similar datatypes
 - Syntax:

   let name_array:[datatype; size_of_array] = [1,2,3,4,5]
